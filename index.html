About Us

At Agreeable Educational Services, Inc., we provide comprehensive school psychology support to help students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Led by an experienced and credentialed school psychologist, our approach combines compassion, evidence-based practice, and effective collaboration with educators and families.

Meet the Psychologist

Angelo Rivera, Ed.S., NCSP

Angelo Rivera is a Nationally Certified School Psychologist (NCSP) with over 9 years of experience supporting students, families, and educators. His approach blends compassion, collaboration, and evidence-based practice to promote student growth and well-being.

Angelo holds a specialist degree in School Psychology and has worked across diverse educational settings, providing assessments, counseling, and consultation services. He founded Agreeable Educational Services, Inc. to help schools and communities access high-quality psychological support with a personal touch.
