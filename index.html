About

Agreeable Educational Services provides compassionate and evidence-based psychological support to help students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our mission is to collaborate with families, educators, and communities to create supportive learning environments where every student can succeed.

Meet the Psychologist

Angelo Rivera, Ed.S., NCSP

Angelo Rivera is a Nationally Certified School Psychologist (NCSP) dedicated to helping students build confidence, resilience, and connections that lead to lifelong learning. His approach integrates data-driven insights with empathy and collaboration to support every student's unique journey.
